# ScoreLab Copywriting Operating Document

## Copy Goal

ScoreLab should sound like an experienced mentor helping a young job seeker, not like AI, HR, or a generic marketing website.

The copy should reduce uncertainty and encourage action.

## Core Positioning

See your resume through a recruiter's eyes.

This line should guide the product, homepage, preview page, report, and paid conversion copy.

## Voice

ScoreLab copy should be:

- Calm
- Direct
- Human
- Encouraging
- Practical
- Honest
- Specific

Avoid sounding:

- Robotic
- Corporate
- Overly motivational
- Judgmental
- Hype-driven
- Fear-based
- Too technical

## Audience Assumption

Write for early-career job seekers who may feel anxious, uncertain, or discouraged.

They are not looking for complex HR language. They want to know:

- Why am I not hearing back?
- What are recruiters seeing?
- What should I fix first?
- Is this worth paying for?
- Can I improve this quickly?

## Preferred Language

Use:

- "may notice"
- "may improve"
- "can help"
- "AI-estimated"
- "recruiter-style feedback"
- "what to fix first"
- "before you apply again"
- "your resume has potential"

Avoid:

- "guaranteed interviews"
- "beat the ATS"
- "perfect resume"
- "bad resume"
- "failed"
- "get hired instantly"
- "10x your career"
- "professional career advice" as a legal claim

## Homepage Copy

Hero headline:

See your resume through a recruiter's eyes.

Subheadline:

Upload your resume and discover what recruiters may notice first before you apply.

CTA:

Analyze My Resume - Free

Support:

No signup required.

Pain section direction:

Applying everywhere can make feedback feel invisible.

Explain that many resumes are ignored not because the candidate is not capable, but because the right signals are hard to find quickly.

## Upload Flow Copy

Upload state:

Great - let's see how recruiters may read your resume.

Loading messages:

- Reading your experience...
- Checking whether your strengths are easy to spot...
- Looking for ATS and recruiter attention signals...
- Finding the few changes that could make the biggest difference...

## Preview Copy

The preview should not only show numbers.

Use a human summary:

Your resume has potential. Here are the top things that may be holding it back today.

CTA:

Unlock My Improvement Plan - $4.99

Value bullets:

- See what recruiters may notice first
- Understand what may be hurting your interview chances
- Get the 5 most important changes to make
- Receive improvement examples
- Download your personalized report

## Report Copy

Every recommendation should answer:

- What we noticed
- Why recruiters care
- What to improve

Keep recommendations fewer and sharper.

Do not give 30 suggestions. Focus on the highest-impact changes.

## Encouragement Standard

Never shame the user.

Do not write:

"This resume is bad."

Write:

"This section has strong potential. Here's how to make it easier for recruiters to understand quickly."

## Disclaimer Language

Use clear disclaimers:

This report is AI-generated and AI-estimated. It is not professional career, legal, or employment advice and does not guarantee interviews or job offers.

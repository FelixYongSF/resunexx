# ScoreLab Pricing Operating Document

## Pricing Goal

ScoreLab pricing should be simple, low-friction, and easy for early-career users to understand.

The first goal is not maximizing revenue per user. The first goal is proving that users will pay for clarity and a focused improvement plan.

## Current Model

- Free preview: $0
- Resume Review / Improvement Plan: $4.99

The paid product should be positioned as:

Unlock My Improvement Plan

Not:

Unlock Full Report

## Free Preview

The free preview should show enough value to build trust:

- Overall score
- ATS score
- Interview readiness level
- Top 3 issues
- Human summary

The preview should not reveal the full improvement plan, rewrite examples, or downloadable PDF.

## Paid Resume Review

Price:

$4.99

Paid value:

- See what recruiters may notice first
- Understand what may be hurting interview chances
- Get the 5 most important changes to make
- Receive rewrite examples
- Download a personalized report

## Future Offers

Target Match:

$14.99-$19.99

Purpose:

Compare a resume against a specific job description and show what to adjust before applying.

Interview Kit:

$29.99-$39.99

Purpose:

Generate a role-specific interview preparation kit from the resume and target job.

## Future Packaging

Possible future ladder:

1. Free recruiter-style preview
2. $4.99 Resume Improvement Plan
3. $14.99-$19.99 Target Match
4. $29.99-$39.99 Interview Kit

Do not add subscriptions until there is evidence of repeated use.

## Pricing Principles

- Keep the first purchase easy.
- Make the value concrete.
- Avoid manipulative urgency.
- Do not overpromise outcomes.
- Do not claim guaranteed interviews.
- Use "may improve" and "can help."
- Price for early-career affordability.

## Conversion Copy

Primary paid CTA:

Unlock My Improvement Plan - $4.99

Supporting line:

Know what to fix before the next application goes out.

## Checkout Requirements

- Use Paddle Checkout.
- Keep checkout simple.
- Verify payment before unlocking the report.
- If Paddle is not configured, show a clear developer error.
- Do not expose paid report content without successful payment verification.

## Pricing Experiments

Future experiments:

- $2.99 first launch price
- $4.99 standard price
- $7.99 with stronger PDF/report packaging
- Bundle: Resume Review + Target Match

Run experiments only after the core flow works reliably.
